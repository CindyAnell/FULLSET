FAQ

Shipping & Handling

How long will it take to receive my order? All sets are handmade to order. Processing typically takes 2–5 business days, followed by 3–5 business days for standard shipping within the U.S. You’ll receive a tracking number as soon as your order ships.

Do you ship internationally? We currently ship within the U.S., but international shipping is coming soon. Stay tuned!

What if my package is delayed or lost? Once your order has shipped, it is in the hands of the carrier. If your package is delayed, please check your tracking first. For lost packages, contact us and we’ll help you file a claim or find a solution.

Sizing

How do I find my nail size? You can measure your nails using a soft measuring tape or the tape method (placing tape across your nail and measuring it). Match your measurements to our sizing chart to find your best fit.

What if I’m between sizes? If you’re in between sizes, we recommend sizing up and gently filing the sides of the nail for a perfect fit.

Do you offer custom sizing? Yes! You can select custom sizing and enter your measurements at checkout for a more tailored fit.

Application

How do I apply press-on nails?

  1. Prep your nails by pushing back cuticles and lightly buffing the surface

  2. Clean nails with alcohol or prep pad

  3. Apply adhesive (glue or tabs)

  4. Press and hold each nail for 20–30 seconds

How long do they last? With nail glue, your set can last 1–2 weeks. With adhesive tabs, wear typically lasts 1–3 days, making them perfect for short-term looks. `

Can I reuse my press-on nails? Yes! When applied with adhesive tabs and removed carefully, your nails can be reused multiple times.

How do I remove them safely? Soak your nails in warm, soapy water or oil for 10–15 minutes, then gently lift from the sides. Never force removal, as this can damage your natural nails.
